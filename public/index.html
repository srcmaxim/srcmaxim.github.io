Software Development Lifecycle Phases Description

Sun, Sep 13, 2020 Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) is a process used by the software industry to design, develop and test high quality softwares. The SDLC aims to produce a high-quality software that meets or exceeds customer expectations, reaches completion within times and cost estimates. The SDLC includes ten phases during which defined IT work products are created or modified. The tenth phase occurs when the system is disposed of and the task performed is either eliminated or transferred to other systems.
